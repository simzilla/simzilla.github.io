What Lies before Us??

Wed 06 July 2016

I believe it is unwise to approach a problem without a plan of attack. In this case my first problem is developing a plan of attack so I obviously need a plan to attack the development of my plan.

I see this thing going pretty sequentially. For instance, I am mostly writing this second post so that I can make sure I have the basics of this Pelican thing under control. Its not brain surgery by any means but I like making sure my platform is sound. This brings me to step 1

This ratty Pelican Blog

I chose Pelican because I know my way around Python and Pelican seems super easy to use and host if I decide to do that. My first in depth post will be writing about how I got this blog up and running.

After that I think I am going to change this theme up. I can't decide if I hate or just don't like it but it definitely needs some tweaks. That should be a fairly quick project...we shall see.

WordPress

Once I am comfortable here, I intend to dive into WordPress. I think its pretty nuts what people charge to do really simple things in WordPress. I want to get into some full blown customization. I am not certain that I have the designers eye for it...I think thats whats really holding me back but I think I can at least get comfortable.

Stops along the Way

I haven't truly taken the time to teach myself CSS and GIT. Those seem like the two most major deficiencies Im facing. CSS I'm fairly familiar with, but I don't know all of the finer points and I think thats important. GIT always gives me trouble but thats because I haven't been forced to use it. I've messed around but I think it is an important tool that I need to get intimate with. Ill probably tackle this first before I do anything else.

Life is getting in the WAY!!!

I kind of have a lot of school-ish things going on right now. Testing for some certs and a few garbage courses. I'm hoping that the threat factor dissipates and I can start focusing on this fun stuff soon. My next post will either be about setting up this blog or getting intimate with GIT. WE SHALL SEE!!!
