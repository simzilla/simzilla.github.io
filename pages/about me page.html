About Me

My background is in computer science, specifically networking and network theory. I don't have alot of practical experience in the field. I've spent the last ten years doing work that has zero to do with computers, unless you count making powerpoint slides. It's really only within the last two years that I have delved back into some technical pursuits. It has been a pretty intense couple of years though, very academically focused. That being said my academic engine is getting burnt out...this is my attempt to have some fun.

I have experience coding in C++, Java, Python, and a few other random languages (LISP, Swift, C#, etc).

My other interests include fitness stuff (running, weightlifting, crossfit), grilling, and the finer things in life...like a nice cold beer.
